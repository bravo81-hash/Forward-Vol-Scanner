How “Copy ONE legs” works

It copies a plain-text checklist: snapshot date/time, strategy rank, and every leg with quantity, expiry, strike and put/call. It does not create a trade automatically. In ONE, create a new position and enter each copied leg at quantity one. For a historical test use the nearest contract ONE actually lists on that date; live TWS legs are already selected from the current listed chain.
